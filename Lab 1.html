Delicious Fried Rice

Easy Homemade Recipe

Fried rice is a quick and tasty dish made with rice, vegetables, eggs, and meat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Chicken pieces
  • Cooking oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Cook the eggs first.
  3. Add vegetables and chicken.
  4. Add cooked rice.
  5. Mix with soy sauce.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes and serve.

Nutrition Information

Nutrition Amount
Calories 450 kcal
Protein 20 g
Fat 15 g
